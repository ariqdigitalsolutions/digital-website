Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about AriQ Digital Solutions services, payments, installations, and support.

1. What services does AriQ Digital Solutions offer?

We provide Starlink solutions, website design, IT support, CCTV installation, digital marketing, business registration assistance, and other technology services across Zimbabwe.

2. How do I pay for services or products?

At AriQ Digital Solutions, we do not require upfront payment for most services. You only pay after we have delivered the work and you are satisfied with the results, giving you confidence and peace of mind throughout the process.

3. Do you supply and install Starlink kits?

Yes. We supply, install, activate, and provide support for Starlink systems for homes, businesses, farms, and remote locations. In most cases, installations are completed within 24 hours of your order being placed, ensuring you get connected as quickly as possible.

4. Do you offer services outside Bulawayo?

Yes. We serve customers nationwide across Zimbabwe.

5. How long does website development take?

Most websites are completed within 1-4 weeks, depending on the project's size and requirements.

6. Can you help register my company or business?

Yes. We assist with company registrations and other business support services.

7. Do you provide ongoing IT support?

Yes. We offer remote and on-site technical support for individuals and businesses.

8. What types of CCTV systems do you install?

We install CCTV solutions for homes, offices, shops, schools, warehouses, and other commercial properties.

9. Do you offer maintenance and after-sales support?

Yes. We provide ongoing support, maintenance, and troubleshooting for services and solutions we deliver.

10. How can I request a quotation?

You can contact us by phone, WhatsApp, email, or through our website contact form for a free, no-obligation quotation.

11. How do I get started?

Simply contact our team, and we will discuss your requirements and recommend the best solution for your needs.
