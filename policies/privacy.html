Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect information submitted through the AriQ website.

Last updated: 13 July 2026

Website policiesPrivacy PolicyTerms and ConditionsCookie NoticeWebsite Disclaimer

1. About this policy

This policy explains how AriQ Digital Solutions handles personal information submitted through this website. It is a practical website policy template and should be reviewed against the business's final operating processes and applicable Zimbabwean law before publication.

2. Information we may receive

When you contact us, we may receive your name, phone number, email address, location, requested service, preferred contact method and the message you provide. The static website does not create customer accounts or store enquiry form submissions in a website database.

3. How enquiries are sent

The contact and order forms prepare the information you entered for delivery through WhatsApp or email. You choose whether to continue in the relevant application. WhatsApp, your email provider and other third-party services process information under their own policies.

4. How information may be used

  • To respond to enquiries and prepare quotations.
  • To arrange assessments, installations or support.
  • To provide requested customer service.
  • To maintain necessary business and transaction records.
  • To protect the website and business from misuse.

5. Sharing and retention

Information should only be shared with staff, contractors or service providers where reasonably necessary to deliver the requested service or comply with legal obligations. AriQ should retain information only for as long as required for the stated purpose and applicable record-keeping duties.

6. Your choices

You may ask AriQ to correct inaccurate contact details or explain how enquiry information is being used. Some records may need to be retained for legal, accounting or legitimate business purposes.

7. Website analytics and cookies

This version of the website uses a local preference cookie only to remember whether the cookie notice was dismissed. If analytics, advertising pixels or embedded third-party tools are added later, this policy and the cookie notice must be updated before deployment.

8. Security

AriQ should use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards. No internet transmission or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure, so sensitive passwords, private keys and payment card details should never be submitted through general enquiry forms.
