Service Overview

A complete, supported approach

AriQ manages the practical work required to turn a Starlink kit into a reliable home or business internet connection. We help with equipment, placement, mounting, cable routing, router setup, coverage planning and handover.

Problems this service solves

  • Slow or unreliable fixed internet
  • No fibre coverage at a home, farm or remote site
  • Weak Wi-Fi coverage inside large buildings
  • Unsafe or temporary dish mounting
  • Difficulty activating or configuring equipment

Main Benefits

What clients gain

  • A cleaner, safer installation
  • Better connection stability
  • Improved indoor Wi-Fi coverage
  • Clear package and support options
  • A local team to contact after handover
